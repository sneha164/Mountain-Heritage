About

Background of Mountain Heritage Nepal

Established in 2007, Mountain Heritage Nepal is a non-governmental and non-profit making organization committed to contribute to the sustainable tourism development and environmental management in Nepal. By getting registration at the District Administration Office- Kathmandu, affiliation at the Social Welfare Council (SWC) - Nepal and also getting Permanent Account Number (PAN) from the Inland Revenue Department, CEST Nepal is a legal entity working in tourism and environment sector in Nepal. Mountain Heritage Nepal, with its focused contribution towards the promotion of sustainable tourism development, has already created a positive image in tourism industry in Nepal.

Mountain Heritage Nepal is also a member of Sustainable Tourism Network (STN), a professional network formed under Nepal Tourism Board. Since 2014, it has been serving as one of the core members of STN.
